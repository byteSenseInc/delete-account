Delete your byteSense account

This page explains how to permanently delete your byteSense account and the data associated with it. byteSense is published by brux Corporation.

Option 1: delete from the app

  1. Open the byteSense app and sign in.
  2. Go to Profile > Account Settings.
  3. Tap Delete Account and confirm with your password.

Option 2: request deletion by email

Email support@bytesense.ai from the email address registered to your account with the subject "Account deletion request". We will verify the request and delete the account.

What gets deleted

Deleting your account permanently removes your account credentials, profile information (name, email address, username), and the sleep and biometric data recorded from your byteSense device.

Records that we are legally required to keep (for example order and payment records) may be retained for as long as the law requires. Data on your paired device can be cleared by resetting the device.
